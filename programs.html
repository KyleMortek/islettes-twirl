Our Programs

From first-time twirlers to national competitors - we have a program for everyone

Find Your Perfect Program

Whether you're picking up a baton for the first time or preparing for national championships, Islettes Twirl Team offers comprehensive programs designed to help you succeed at every level.

Tiny Twirlers (Ages 5-8)

Introduction to Baton Twirling

Our Tiny Twirlers program is designed for young children who are new to baton twirling. In a fun, encouraging environment, students learn the basics of baton handling, simple tricks, and performance skills.

What You'll Learn:

  • Basic baton grips and hand positions
  • Simple spins and tricks
  • Coordination and rhythm development
  • Introduction to routines and choreography
  • Performance confidence and stage presence

Class Details:

  • Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Duration: 1 hour per session
  • Class Size: Maximum 12 students
  • Cost: $120/month
  • Equipment: Beginner baton provided
Enroll Now

Junior Performers (Ages 9-13)

Building Advanced Skills

The Junior Performers program is for students who have mastered the basics and are ready to take their skills to the next level. Students work on more complex tricks, longer routines, and begin preparing for optional competition opportunities.

What You'll Learn:

  • Advanced baton manipulation and tricks
  • Multiple baton work
  • Choreography development
  • Performance technique and expression
  • Introduction to competitive formats (optional)
  • Team routines and synchronization

Class Details:

  • Schedule: Monday & Wednesday, 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
  • Duration: 1.5 hours per session
  • Class Size: Maximum 15 students
  • Cost: $160/month
  • Prerequisites: Completion of beginner program or evaluation
Enroll Now

Competitive Team (Ages 10+)

Elite Performance Training

Our Competitive Team is for dedicated athletes who want to compete at regional and national levels. Team members receive intensive training, participate in competitions throughout the year, and represent Islettes at prestigious events.

What's Included:

  • Advanced technical training
  • Custom routine choreography
  • Competition preparation and coaching
  • Team building activities
  • Performance opportunities throughout the year
  • Individual skill development plans
  • Mental preparation and sports psychology

Team Details:

  • Schedule: Tuesday, Thursday, Friday 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Additional: Saturday practices before competitions
  • Duration: 2 hours per session
  • Cost: $250/month plus competition fees
  • Prerequisites: Tryout or coach evaluation required
  • Commitment: Full season (September - June)

Competition Schedule:

  • Local competitions: 4-6 per season
  • Regional championships: 1-2 per season
  • National championships: 1 per season (qualification required)

Private & Semi-Private Lessons

Personalized Instruction

Take your skills to the next level with one-on-one coaching or small group instruction. Private lessons are perfect for athletes preparing for specific competitions, working on challenging skills, or wanting individualized attention.

Private Lesson Options:

  • Individual (1-on-1): $60 per hour
  • Semi-Private (2 students): $40 per student per hour
  • Small Group (3-4 students): $30 per student per hour

What to Expect:

  • Customized lesson plans based on your goals
  • Video analysis and feedback
  • Flexible scheduling
  • All skill levels welcome

Summer Intensive Camp

Week-Long Training Camp

Our annual Summer Intensive Camp is a week-long program featuring guest coaches, specialized workshops, and intensive training. Perfect for athletes who want to make significant progress during the summer break.

Camp Highlights:

  • 4 hours of training per day
  • Guest coaches from national programs
  • Specialized workshops (tricks, performance, choreography)
  • Small group instruction
  • End-of-camp showcase performance
  • Camp t-shirt and swag included

Camp Details:

  • Dates: July 10-14, 2026
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM daily
  • Ages: 8-16 years
  • Cost: $350 (early bird: $300 before June 1)
  • Lunch: Bring your own or pre-order ($30/week)
