Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll my child?

You can enroll by filling out the contact form above, calling our office, or visiting during office hours. We'll discuss your child's age, experience level, and interests to recommend the best program.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for new students. This gives your child a chance to experience baton twirling and meet our coaches before making a commitment.

What should my child bring to the first class?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and bring water. We provide batons for beginners. As students progress, we'll recommend appropriate equipment for purchase.

How do I join the competitive team?

Competitive team membership requires a tryout or evaluation with our coaches. Tryouts are typically held in September. Contact us to schedule an evaluation or learn about the next tryout date.

What are the costs involved?

Monthly tuition varies by program level (see our Programs page for details). Additional costs may include competition fees, costumes, travel for competitive team members, and optional events. We offer payment plans and some scholarship opportunities.

Can adults join?

While our primary focus is on youth programming, we occasionally offer adult workshops and classes. Contact us to express your interest and we'll notify you when adult programs are available.
