About Islettes Twirl Team

Building champions through dedication, artistry, and excellence since 2010

Our Story

Islettes Twirl Team was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate coaches and parents who wanted to create a supportive environment where young athletes could develop their baton twirling skills while building confidence, discipline, and lifelong friendships.

What started as a small recreational program has grown into one of the region's most respected competitive twirling teams. Our members have earned numerous state, regional, and national titles, but more importantly, they've developed character traits that serve them well beyond the performance floor.

Our Mission & Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every performance, practice, and interaction. Our goal is to help each athlete reach their full potential.

Teamwork

Success comes through collaboration. We support each other, celebrate achievements together, and grow stronger as a team.

Respect

We treat everyone with dignity and respect - teammates, coaches, competitors, and judges. Good sportsmanship is fundamental to who we are.

Dedication

Achievement requires commitment. We encourage consistent effort, regular practice, and a willingness to push beyond comfort zones.

Fun

While we work hard, we also believe in enjoying the journey. Baton twirling should be challenging, rewarding, and fun.

Community

We're more than a team - we're a family. Our community supports each other on and off the performance floor.

Our Facilities

We train in a state-of-the-art 5,000 square foot facility designed specifically for baton twirling and dance. Our space features:

Professional Floor

Spring-loaded performance floor designed to reduce impact and prevent injuries while providing the perfect surface for twirling.

Mirror Walls

Full-length mirrors allow athletes to perfect their technique and observe their form during practice.

Audio/Visual Equipment

Professional sound system and video recording equipment to help athletes review and improve their performances.

Comfortable Seating

Viewing area for parents and guests to watch practices and performances in comfort.
