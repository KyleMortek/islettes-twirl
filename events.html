Event Information

Attending Performances

Most of our showcase performances require tickets purchased in advance. Tickets typically range from $10-15 per person. Children under 3 are free.

What to Expect: Performances last approximately 2 hours with a 15-minute intermission. Photography is allowed but please silence all devices during performances.

Competition Spectators

Family and friends are welcome at competitions! Spectator passes can be purchased at the door or in advance. Fees vary by competition.

Competition Etiquette: Please remain quiet during performances, flash photography is not allowed, and video recording may be restricted. Check specific competition rules.

Team Member Participation

Participation in performances and competitions varies by program level. Competitive team members are expected to participate in all team events.

Questions? Contact your coach or our front office for specific event requirements and details.
